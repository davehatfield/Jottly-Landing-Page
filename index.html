What is Jott.ly?

It is the best thing to happen to writing since the pen! Well not exactly, but we do want to give you all the simplicity of grabbing a pen and paper and scribbling down your thoughts. But with all the mighty powers of the internet to make those scribbles better. We give you the tools to write and share your thought, ideas, stories, and work. As well as the tools to annotate and discuss your writing. Then be able keep all of your writing in one place. You can import documents you wrote ouside of Jott.ly or export documents written in Jott.ly to be used anywhere. We hope to make it easier than ever to express yourself and work together. Plus it’s as free as the pen you stole from the bank...but with Jott.ly it’s guilt free.
